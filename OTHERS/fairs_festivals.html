FAIRS AND FESTIVALS IN KERALA

Thrissur Pooram

During the month of Medam in the Malayalam calendar, the entire of Kerala gets ready to celebrate an annual Hindu Temple Festival called the ThrissurPooram. The largest and the most popular among all the Temple Festivals in Kerala, this celebration marks the rising of the moon with the ‘Pooram’ star!

Attukal Pongala

AttukalPongala or the ‘Festival of Women’ is among the most devout festivals in Kerala. Celebrated mainly in the AttukalBhagavathi Temple in Thiruvananthapuram, this holy festival has been declared as the ‘Largest Single Gathering of Women’ by the Guinness Society!

Boat Festival

Boat Race or ‘VallamKalli’ is one of the most vigorously celebrated festivals in Kerala. An annual festival in God’s Own Country, it is a traditional boat race that is held during Kerala’s harvest festival ‘Onam’, and is celebrated by locals from all religious background.

Martial Arts

Considered among the oldest and most scientific martial arts in the world, Kalaripayattu was developed in Kerala. Lauded as the pride of Kerala, it is acknowledged and respected across the world.
