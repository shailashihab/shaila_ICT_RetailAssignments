Sri-Padmanabhaswamy-Temple

If you want to see the exquisite Dravidian style of architecture, then plan a trip to the well known Sree PadmanabhaswamyTemple in Thiruvananthapuram, the capital city. This richest temple in the country is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and believed to be built in the 8th century. Sree PadmanabhaswamyTemple is one of the 108 Vishnu temples.
