MAJOR WATERFALLS

Athirappally and Vazhachal

Kerala, an amazingly beautiful green land is blessed with wonderful waterfalls. Athirappilly Falls is one of the most beautiful and popular way among them. Athirappilly is said to be the land of rivers and waterfalls. Athirappilly waterfall located at the entrance of Sholayar ranges at a distance of about 78 km from Kochi draws thousands of tourists from every part of the country.

Palaruvi

Palaruvi waterfalls one of the most wonderful waterfalls in Kerala is in the Cashew town Kollam. The water in here is said to be medicated and is preventive against many diseases. Water cascading down a height of 300 feet across the rocky mountains is a wonderful scene. The journey to Palaruvi itself is amazing with the scenic beauty.

Thusharagiri

Cuddled in the western ghats the gurgling waterfalls of Thusharagiri show a peculiar relationship between land and water.This popular waterfall is situated 50 km east of Kozhikode. Two stream originating from the western ghats meet to form the river Chalippuzha.
