ADVENTURE TOURISM

Kerala offers some of the most diverse kinds of adventure sports in India that are a must try for an adventure enthusiast. Let us look at the popular adventure sports practiced in Kerala.
  • Parasailing – Fly Above The Mighty Sea

    LOCATIONS: Kovalam and Alappuzha Beach

  • Cycling – Race Through The Trails

    LOCATIONS: Kottayam to Vagamon, Chalakudy to Valparai, Devikulam to Munnar, Alappuzha to Changanassery
  • Paragliding – Feel Like Superman

    LOCATIONS: Vagamon & Varkala
  • Trekking – Of Natural Beauty And Exploration

    LOCATIONS: Chembra Peak in Wayanad, Kolukkumalai and Meesapulimala in Munnar, mountain ranges near Thenmala
  • Tree House Stay – For A Soothing Time

    LOCATIONS: Wayanad, Munnar and Thekkady
  • Kayaking – The Best Way To Enjoy Beauty

    LOCATIONS: Alappuzha Beach
  • Snorkeling – Explore The Underwater

    LOCATIONS: Thiruvankulam, Kochi
  • Wildlife Safari – Exploring The Wild

    LOCATIONS: Periyar NP, Silent Valley NP, Eravikulam NP, idukki WildLife Sanctuary, Aralam WildLife Sanctuary
  • Bamboo Rafting – A Unique Sport

    LOCATIONS: Periyar National Park, Thekkady
  • Scuba Diving – Dive Into The Depths

    LOCATIONS: Kovalam & Kochi
  • Mountain Climbing – A Tedious And Rushing Task

    LOCATIONS: Rajamalai, Eravikulam National Park, Analudi Peak, Munnar
  • Swimming – Bath In The Arabian Sea

    LOCATIONS: Kovalam Beach, Alappuzha Beach, Marari Beach, Vizhinjam Beach
